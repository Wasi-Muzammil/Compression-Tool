**About File Compressor Tool**

**Overview**

File Compressor Tool is a powerful and user-friendly application designed to compress a wide range of file formats, including audio, video, images, and documents. Our tool uses advanced compression algorithms to reduce the size of files while maintaining their quality, making it an essential utility for individuals and organizations looking to save storage space and bandwidth.

* **Audio Files**: WAV
* **Video Files**: MP4, GIF, MOV
* **Image Files**: PNG, JPG, JPEG
* **Document Files**: TXT, PDF, DOCX
* **Spreadsheet Files**: CSV
